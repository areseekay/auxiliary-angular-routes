This is the Lazy Child Home

This Component/Module was loaded as lazy module -> https://angular.io/guide/lazy-loading-ngmodules. It has it's own Router Outlet and structure. But still it can have named router outlets, which behave similar to a named outlet in a usual loaded module. You find two separate router-outlets 'left' and 'right' (check it out via dev-tools of your browser). Once you click on the following buttons, you see in action where it will render the content
